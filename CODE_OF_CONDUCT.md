# Code of Conduct

BySentinel follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.
- Avoid public disclosure of security issues or sensitive data.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing,
public sharing of private security reports and sustained disruption are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions or block
participants who violate this code. Security reports should be handled privately
according to [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
